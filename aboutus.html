We are GWC - Girls Who Code

We're Girls Who Code and we have worked really hard over the past year to make this product work/happen. Our task was to create a product which solves a problem. The problem that we thought of was how girls don't really get known as "gamers" and how girls get stereotyped as the damsel in distress. We took on this challenge by creating a game where a girl is the main character. In this game, we have a girl who goes through a series of challenges that are presented through dreams. The girl is faced with multiple challenges which requires the girl to not depend on someone else to come and save her. You can also customize the girl and the girl also has her own pet.
